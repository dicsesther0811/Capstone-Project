# AI-Based Food Analysis and Personalized Diet Recommendation System

> AI 기반 음식 분석 및 개인 맞춤 식단 추천 시스템

---

## 1. Project Overview

### 1.1 Project Title

**AI-Based Food Analysis and Personalized Diet Recommendation System**

### 1.2 Project Goal

The goal of this project is to develop a service that analyzes food images to identify ingredients and provide nutritional information, and recommends personalized diets based on the user's health condition and dietary patterns.

Through this service, users can better understand their eating habits and make food choices suited to their health conditions.

---

## 2. Target Users

This service targets a diverse range of users who need dietary management, not limited to any specific group.

### 2.1 Diet Management Users

Users who want to maintain healthy eating habits or manage their weight.

- People on a diet
- People aiming for muscle gain or weight loss through exercise
- General users who want to maintain a healthy lifestyle

### 2.2 Users with Medical Conditions

Users for whom dietary management is critical due to specific health conditions.

- Diabetes
- Hypertension
- Hyperlipidemia

### 2.3 Users in Unfamiliar Food Environments

Users living in food environments they are not accustomed to.

- International students
- Overseas residents
- Users adapting to a new food culture

---

## 3. Problem Statement

Various dietary management services exist today, but they share the following limitations.

### 3.1 Calorie-Centric Services

Many services focus primarily on calorie counting, without providing sufficient information about ingredient composition or nutritional balance.

### 3.2 Insufficient Consideration of Individual Health Conditions

Most services offer diet-focused features without adequately accounting for users' health conditions.

- Low-sugar diet management for diabetic patients
- Low-sodium diet management for hypertensive patients
- Low-fat diet management for patients with hyperlipidemia

### 3.3 Lack of Long-term Dietary Pattern Analysis

Existing services mostly provide only daily meal logging. The ability to analyze long-term dietary habits to identify issues such as overconsumption or chronic deficiency of specific nutrients is limited.

- Overconsumption of specific nutrients
- Persistent deficiency of specific nutrients
- Unbalanced dietary patterns

### 3.4 Insufficient Personalized Food Recommendation

Existing services focus on meal logging and nutritional information, but lack concrete recommendation features to help users decide what to eat.

- Meal logging
- Nutritional information provision

### 3.5 Insufficient Food Composition Information

Existing food recognition services focus on identifying the name of a food, and often do not provide sufficient information about ingredients and food components.

- Ingredients
- Food components

## 6. Core Features

### 6.1 Food Analysis

When a user uploads a food photo, the AI analyzes the image and provides the following information:

- Food type recognition
- Ingredient analysis
- Nutritional information (Ingredients, Calories, Protein, Carbohydrates, Fat, Sodium)

### 6.2 Dietary Pattern Analysis

User meal log data is analyzed to identify long-term dietary habits and patterns.

- Insufficient protein intake
- Excessive carbohydrate intake
- Excessive sodium intake
- Insufficient vegetable intake

### 6.3 Personalized Food Recommendation

Customized food recommendations are made based on the user's health condition, dietary patterns, and food preferences.

| Condition | Recommended Diet |
|---|---|
| Diabetes | Low-sugar diet |
| Hypertension | Low-sodium diet |
| Hyperlipidemia | Low-fat diet |

---

## 7. User Flow

The service operates through the following steps:

1. User profile input (health condition, dietary goal, food preferences)
2. Food photo upload
3. AI food analysis (ingredient and nutritional information provided)
4. Meal log saved
5. Dietary pattern analysis
6. Personalized food recommendations delivered

## 8. Key Technology

### 8.1 Computer Vision

Food images are analyzed to identify food types and ingredients.

### 8.2 Nutrition Database

A nutrition database is used to link food ingredients with their corresponding nutritional information.

### 8.3 Recommendation System

Personalized food recommendations are generated based on the user's health condition, dietary patterns, and food preferences.

This service is not simply a calorie-logging app — it is an **AI-powered personalized dietary management service**.
